Safe Weed Disposal

  • Tips!

    • Compost Bin: make the habit of using compost bins to dispose weed seedlings.
    • Vigilance: They say one year's seeds equals seven years' weed.
    • Fire: Getting a “Weed burner” or a “Flame weeder” from a nearby supplier can be a good start.
    • Improper Disposal: Never dump garden waste over the back fence or in bushland.
    • Report: Ensure reporting any weed infested vacant blocks to the local council.
