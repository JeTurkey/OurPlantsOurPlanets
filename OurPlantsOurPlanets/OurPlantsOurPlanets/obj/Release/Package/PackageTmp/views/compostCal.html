How to Compost

What will you need

01

Nitrogen

Green Ingredients

Fruits and vegetable scraps, grass and weed clippings, leaves, tea, lint, coffee grounds, hair

02

Carbon

Brown Ingredients

Sawdust, wood chips, dead leaves, branches and twigs, cotton, shredded paper and cardboard and nutshells

03

Water

Keep it moist, but not wet. Keep turning.It is important to get air into the compost heap.

Composting Steps

Select a dry shady spot near a water source. Ideal size of compost area is 3 ft. deep, 3 ft. wide and 3 ft. tall.
Add layering. Try and keep the ratio roughly 3 browns: 1 green. Chop and shred larger pieces of material.
Keep the compost moist, but not too wet. Moisture triggers the breakdown of organic matter.
Regularly turn your compost mixture to provide aeration and eliminating foul odour.
As the material breaksdown, the pile gets warm. It's working, now wait and watch.
When the compost becomes dry, it's fully cooked and ready to feed your garden. Add 4-6 inches of compost to your flower bed and pots.
