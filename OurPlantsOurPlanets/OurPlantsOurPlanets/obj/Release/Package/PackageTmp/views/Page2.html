Did You Know?

Garden Escapees

1831

Out of the 2,779 introduced plant species in Australia an alarming 1,831 (66%) are garden escapees.

Australian Flora

24000

There are an astounding 24,000 species of Australian flora, making it one of our most prized assets, 15% of these are impacted by weed.

Economic Loss

900M

The Victorian Government spends $900 million per year on managing the impact of weeds.
